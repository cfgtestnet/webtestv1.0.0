About

CircleFloat Games is a start-up game company that envisions to meld NFT,  Blockchain Technology, Metaverse, Augmented Reality (AR), and Virtual Reality (VR). With its ever-evolving flagship 3D Games, the company will take on the industry one step at a time.

Our Mission 

 ​CircleFloat Games will integrate Augmented Reality Technology with the current  game, and will see to it that Virtual Reality Technology will be integrated soon, after  further development. Making the game fun, safe, and sustainable is the primary goal of  the company, and play-to-earn system will be implemented along the way to  further open the possibilities within the game.

Our Vision 

NFT based gaming where CFG  flagship games meet the real world via Augmented Reality and Virtual Reality platforms. A sustainable play-to-earn economy with fun gameplay, and real world token utility.
